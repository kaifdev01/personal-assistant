## 💡 Pro Tips

### Food Tracking:
- **Log immediately** - Don't wait until end of day
- **Be honest** - Accurate tracking = better results
- **Use search** - 100+ foods in database
- **Custom entry** - For homemade meals, estimate calories
- **Check AI tips** - Read recommendations after each meal

### Calorie Goals:
- **Fat loss**: Eat 300-500 below goal
- **Maintenance**: Match your goal
- **Muscle gain**: Eat 200-300 above goal
- **Adjust weekly** - Based on progress

### Best Practices:
- **Weigh yourself weekly** - Same time, same conditions
- **Track everything** - Even small snacks count
- **Stay hydrated** - Water helps with fat loss
- **Sleep 7-8 hours** - Body burns fat during sleep
- **Be consistent** - 80% consistency beats 100% perfection
