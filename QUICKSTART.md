## 🎯 First Steps

### 1. **Dashboard** (Home)
- See your daily overview
- Check off prayers as you complete them
- Log water intake (click the water droplets)
- View today's tasks
- Track your daily score

### 2. **Start Your First Workout** 🏋️
1. Click **Fitness** in sidebar
2. Expand any workout (click the arrow)
3. Click **"Start Workout"** button
4. You'll enter **Live Workout Session Mode**:
   - Read exercise instructions
   - See target muscles & benefits
   - Click "Start Set" when ready
   - Complete the reps
   - Click "Complete Set"
   - Auto rest timer starts
   - Repeat for all sets
   - Move to next exercise automatically

### 3. **Track Prayers** 🕌
1. Click **Prayer** in sidebar
2. Click each prayer box to mark as complete
3. Track Quran pages read (+ / - buttons)
4. View weekly prayer stats

### 4. **Manage Tasks** ✅
1. Click **Tasks** in sidebar
2. Click **"+ Add Task"**
3. Fill in title, category, priority, due date
4. Click task circle to mark complete
5. Edit or delete with icons on right

### 5. **Track Habits** 🎯
1. Click **Habits** in sidebar
2. Each habit has smart input:
   - **Water/Steps**: Enter number
   - **Workout/No Sugar**: Toggle button
   - **Sleep/Screen time**: Enter hours
3. Progress bars show completion

### 6. **View Analytics** 📊
1. Click **Analytics** in sidebar
2. Switch between tabs:
   - Overview (all metrics)
   - Prayers (consistency chart)
   - Fitness (calories + water)
   - Weight (progress line)

### 7. **Check Schedule** 📅
1. Click **Schedule** in sidebar
2. View **Smart Daily Routine** (optimized timeline)
3. Switch to **Weekly Schedule** tab
4. See heat-aware workout recommendations

### 8. **AI Assistant** 🤖
1. Click **AI Assistant** in sidebar
2. Read personalized suggestions
3. View 7-day workout plan
4. Check budget meal plans
5. Get motivational coaching

### 9. **Settings** ⚙️
1. Click **Settings** in sidebar
2. Toggle **Dark Mode** (top switch)
3. Adjust water goal
4. Export data (backup)
5. Reset all data (if needed)

## 🔥 Pro Tips

### Workout Session Mode
- **Pause anytime** - Life happens, pause and resume
- **Skip exercises** - Not feeling it? Skip to next
- **Skip rest** - Feeling strong? Skip rest timer
- **Read form tips** - Prevent injury with proper form
- **Track progress** - See Exercise X/Y, Set X/Y at top

### Daily Routine
- **Morning (6-7 AM)**: Workout before uni (cooler temp)
- **8 AM - 2 PM**: University
- **2-3 PM**: Lunch + quick study
- **3-9 PM**: Work
- **9-10 PM**: Evening walk or workout
- **10 PM+**: Wind down, no screens

### Heat Management (35-45°C)
- Workout **before 7 AM** or **after 9 PM**
- Drink **12-16 glasses** of water (not just 8)
- Stay indoors during peak heat
- Add pinch of salt to water (electrolytes)

### Fat Loss Tips
- **HIIT workouts** burn more than cardio
- **Fasted morning workout** = max fat burn
- **Consistency > intensity** - 20 min daily beats 2 hours once
- **Track everything** - What gets measured gets managed
- **Sleep 7-8 hours** - Body burns fat during sleep

3. Save the file somewhere safe

## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: Will I lose data if I close the browser?**
A: No! Everything is saved to localStorage automatically.

**Q: Can I use this on multiple devices?**
A: Currently no sync. Each device has its own data. Export/import to transfer.

**Q: Do I need internet?**
A: No! Works 100% offline after first load.

**Q: Can I customize workouts?**
A: Currently 4 pre-built workouts. Custom builder coming in future update.

**Q: How accurate are calorie estimates?**
A: Estimates based on average 75kg person. Actual burn varies by intensity and body weight.
